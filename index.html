About

Hi! I am a 2nd year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of South Carolina. I am part of the AI Institute and the CSCE Department, working with Dr. Vignesh Narayanan since Fall 2024. Before that, I completed my Master's in Data Science from Chennai Mathematical Institute, India in 2023 and my Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Calcutta in 2021. My research interests are broadly centered on applications of AI in robotics and cyber-physical systems. Specifically, I work on developing adaptive controllers with a focus on safety, optimality, and resource efficiency using machine learning and reinforcement learning techniques. I am also interested in integrating planning and control, with recent work exploring information-efficient multi-agent path finding and hybrid frameworks that combine decentralized planning with lightweight centralized coordination to improve scalability, safety, and execution quality in robotic systems.
