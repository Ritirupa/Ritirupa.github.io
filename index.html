About me

Brief introduction

I am a PhD student working at the intersection of robotics and intelligent systems. My research combines machine learning, control, and planning to make robotic systems safer, more adaptive, and more useful in real-world settings.

I enjoy working on methods that support collaboration between humans and robots, especially when reliability, safety, and interpretability matter.
